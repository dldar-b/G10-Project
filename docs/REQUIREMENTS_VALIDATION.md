# ✅ Requirements Validation and Risk Analysis
**Role 6 – Dldar Bahri**

## Validation Overview
The goal of this stage was to ensure that all requirements were **complete**, **consistent**, and **testable** before moving to design and development.

### Validation Methods:
- **Prototyping:** To confirm that the system meets user needs and expectations.  
- **Team Reviews:** Each requirement was reviewed by the group to verify clarity and completeness.  
- **Test Cases:** Each function was mapped to a test case to ensure it could be verified later.

---

## Risks Identified:
1. **Unclear or Incomplete Requirements:**  
   → Could cause confusion or incorrect implementation.  
2. **Frequent Requirement Changes from Client:**  
   → May delay development and design phases.

---

## Mitigation Strategies:
- Conduct regular **review meetings** with the client.  
- Maintain a **change control log** to record all modifications.  
- Validate each requirement before it proceeds to the design stage.

---

## Conclusion:
After review, the requirements were found to be mostly **clear, complete, and consistent**.  
The team established an effective validation and risk management plan to ensure project quality and success.
